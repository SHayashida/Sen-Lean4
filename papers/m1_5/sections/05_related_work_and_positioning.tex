\section{Related Work and Positioning}

The repository decision implemented here is deliberately orthogonal to paper content.
It separates manuscript management from shared research infrastructure: M1 remains stable, M1.5 receives its own workspace, and the shared codebase continues to support cumulative development.

TruthWeave may be introduced later as an audit layer for M1.5 if stronger claim, provenance, or reviewer-packet workflows become necessary.
For now, the repository keeps its existing manuscript and artifact workflow as the source of truth and avoids a disruptive migration while M1 is active.
