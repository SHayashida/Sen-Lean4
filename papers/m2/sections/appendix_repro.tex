\section{Reproducibility Note}
\label{sec:repro}

This manuscript is grounded in kernel-checked Lean modules and repository-local reproducibility artifacts rather than new solver experiments.
The main evidence sources used in the text are:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/ObstructionBasis.lean} --- witness extraction and O2/O3/O4 classification.
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/ObstructionProfiles.lean} --- generic prefix rankings and ambient two-ranking profiles.
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/ObstructionSoundness.lean} --- exact support cardinalities, shape soundness, and outcome refutation.
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/ObstructionBridge.lean} --- the generic bridge theorems \(\mathtt{sen\_outcome\_obstruction\_of\_UN\_MINLIB}\) and \(\mathtt{sen\_impossibility\_from\_obstruction\_basis}\).
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/Lifting/ImpossibilityLift.lean} --- the legacy compatibility corollary \(\mathtt{sen\_impossibility\_lifts}\).
\item \code{SocialChoiceAtlas.lean} --- the root import lines that wire the bridge and compatibility theorem into the default \(\mathtt{lake\ build}\).
\item \code{scripts/ci\_m2\_smoke.sh} --- the M2 smoke gate for bridge imports, theorem anchors, and placeholder scans.
\item \code{docs/m2\_scope\_wall.md} --- the scope wall.
\item \code{docs/no\_cycle\_interpretation\_note.md} and \code{docs/axiom\_semantics\_scaling.md} --- the prior repository notes that establish why the short-cycle encoding does not transfer past \(m = 4\).
\item \code{scripts/check\_acyclicity\_short\_cycles.py} --- the finite check that audits the short-cycle encoding at the base case.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Build commands}
\label{sec:build-commands}

The narrow build commands that exercise the bridge and compatibility theorem are:
\begin{quote}
\code{lake env lean SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/ObstructionBridge.lean}
\end{quote}
\begin{quote}
\code{lake env lean SocialChoiceAtlas/Sen/Lifting/ImpossibilityLift.lean}
\end{quote}

The M2 smoke gate is:
\begin{quote}
\code{./scripts/ci\_m2\_smoke.sh}
\end{quote}

The full default kernel build is:
\begin{quote}
\code{lake build}
\end{quote}

The manuscript itself builds with:
\begin{quote}
\code{make -C papers/m2 pdf}
\end{quote}
