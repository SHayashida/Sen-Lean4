\section{Background And Running Lineage}

This section will situate M4 in the existing Sen24 artifact line without
turning the scaffold into a full literature review or a new theorem statement.
The purpose is to explain which earlier milestones supply background and which
claims remain outside M4.

M1 separates proof, audit, witness, and assumptions in the finite Sen24
evidence layer. M1.5 shows that valid impossibility evidence does not determine
a canonical raw repair report. M2 contains the semantic obstruction bridge and
the general Sen theorem; those results are background, not new M4 claims. M3
gives sufficient conditions for grouped repair reporting. M4 focuses on claim
boundaries for the declared finite artifact.
